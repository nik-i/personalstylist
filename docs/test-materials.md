## 1. Recruiting message (network / community / social)

> **Subject / hook:** Want a free personal styling session? (I'm testing something)
>
> Hi [name] — I'm building a personal styling tool and I'm looking for a few people to try an early version with me. It's totally free, and you get a real styling session out of it:
>
> - I'll look at your wardrobe and send you 2–3 outfit ideas using clothes **you already own**.
> - If you've got something coming up (a trip, an event, a new job), I'll tell you exactly what's **missing** and the 3 pieces worth buying to fill the gap.
>
> It takes ~20–30 min of your time to set up, then we'll chat for 15 min about what worked. No cost, no obligation, and your photos stay private between us.
>
> Up for it? I can only take ~6 people for this round.

**Screening line (reply with):** "Quick q first — roughly how often do you shop for clothes online, and is there anything coming up you'd want to dress for?"
*(Skip anyone who says they don't really think about clothes.)*

---

## 2. Intake request (send once they say yes)

> Amazing — thank you! Here's everything I need. Send it however's easiest (album link, photos in chat, or this form). Don't overthink it.
>
> **A. A few of your best looks** 📸
> 3–5 photos of you in outfits you *love* and feel great in. These tell me your taste.
>
> **B. Your wider wardrobe** 👗
> Photos of the rest of what you own — pull a batch from your camera roll over the last several months, *or* snap a few quick shots of your closet/drawers. The more I can see, the better the advice. (This is the bit that takes the most time — but it's what makes the whole thing actually useful.)
>
> **C. A few quick details**
> - Usual sizes (tops / bottoms / dresses / shoes):
> - Which body shape sounds closest? *[attach the labeled silhouette image]* — or just say "not sure":
> - Anything you love to **show off**? Anything you'd rather **draw attention away from**? *(totally optional — skip if you'd rather not)*:
> - Where are you based (city/country)?
> - Rough budget per piece, and any favorite brands/shops *(optional)*:
>
> **D. What are we dressing for?**
> Is there a specific occasion or goal — a trip, event, new job, season change — or are you just looking to refresh things? And: *what's your biggest wardrobe frustration right now?*

*Privacy note to include:* "Your photos are only seen by me for this session and won't be shared or used for anything else."

## 3. Delivery template (what you send back)

> **Hi [name] — here's your styling session ✨**
>
> ### Part 1 — Outfits from what you already own
> *(2–3 looks built entirely from their wardrobe)*
> - **Look 1 — [occasion/vibe]:** [pieces] — *why it works for you:* [1 line]
> - **Look 2 — …**
> - **Look 3 — …**
>
> ### Part 2 — Your gap for [their goal]
> *(only for participants with a live goal)*
> - **What you're missing:** [the gap, grounded in what they own — and call out anything they already have so they don't re-buy]
> - **The piece to target:** [general style, e.g. "a structured blazer in a warm neutral that skims the hip"]
> - **3 to consider:**
>   1. [Product + link] — [price] — [why this one]
>   2. [Product + link] — [price]
>   3. [Product + link] — [price]
>   *(In their retailers if given; otherwise one each across low/mid/higher price.)*

## 4. Follow-up interview script (~15 min)

Ask, don't lead. Let silences sit.

1. **Onboarding:** "Walk me through getting your wardrobe to me — what was that like? Anything annoying or where you nearly gave up?"
2. **Daily value:** "The outfits from clothes you own — how useful, 1 to 10? Would you open something like this daily or weekly? Better or worse than anything you use now?"
3. **Gap recs:** "Did the missing-pieces advice feel right? Anything I suggested you actually already own?"
4. **The truth test:** "Would you buy any of the three? …Would you buy it *now*?" *(then, later)* "Did you end up buying anything?" → "Would you send a pic if you wear it?"
5. **Willingness to pay:** "If this were an app, what would you expect to pay — free, one-off, subscription?"
6. **Open:** "What would make you *not* use this?"

→ Log answers in the results template in `validation-test-plan.md`.
